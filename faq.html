Frequently Aseked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Reshie

General Questions

What is Reshie?

Reshie is an e-commerce platform founded by students of Pamantasan ng Cabuyao, offering a wide range of products with a focus on technology gadgets and accessories.

How can I contact customer support?

You can contact our customer support team via email at support@reshie.com, by phone at (+63) 123 456 7891, or by visiting our Contact page.

What are your business hours?

Our customer service team is available Monday to Friday from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM, and Saturday from 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays and holidays.

Account Questions

How do I create an account?

To create an account, click on the "My Account" button in the top right corner of the website and select "Register". Fill in the required information and click "Register" to complete the process.

How can I reset my password?

If you've forgotten your password, click on "My Account", then "Login", and select "Forgot Password". Enter your email address, and we'll send you instructions to reset your password.

How do I update my profile information?

Once logged in, click on "My Account" and select "Profile". Here you can update your personal information, shipping addresses, and payment methods.

Product Questions

How do I know if a product is in stock?

Product availability is displayed on the product page. If a product is in stock, you'll see "In Stock" and can add it to your cart. If it's out of stock, you'll see "Out of Stock".

Do you offer product warranties?

Yes, all our products come with a standard manufacturer's warranty. Specific warranty information is available on individual product pages.

How can I find products on your website?

You can browse products by category, use the search bar at the top of the page, or check out our featured products on the homepage.

Orders & Shipping Questions

How can I track my order?

Once your order has been shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also track your order by logging into your account and viewing your order history.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days within the Philippines. Express shipping options are available at checkout for faster delivery.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we only ship within the Philippines. We plan to expand our shipping options to other countries in the future.

Payment Questions

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards, GCash, PayMaya, and cash on delivery (COD) for orders within Metro Manila.

Is it safe to enter my credit card information on your website?

Yes, we use industry-standard SSL encryption to protect your payment information. Your credit card details are never stored on our servers.

When will I be charged for my order?

Your payment is processed immediately when you place your order. For COD orders, payment is collected upon delivery.

Returns & Refunds Questions

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 7 days of delivery for products in their original condition and packaging. Some items, such as earphones and personal care products, cannot be returned for hygiene reasons.

How do I request a return or refund?

To request a return or refund, log into your account, go to "Orders," select the order you want to return, and click "Request Return." Follow the instructions to complete your return request.

How long does it take to process a refund?

Once we receive your returned item, we will inspect it and process your refund within 3-5 business days. It may take additional days for the refund to appear in your account, depending on your payment method.

Still Have Questions?

If you couldn't find the answer to your question, please contact our customer support team.
